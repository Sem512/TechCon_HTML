History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2001 as a small meetup for technology enthusiasts and has grown into one of the most anticipated annual tech events. Over the years, TechCon has brought together innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs to discuss the latest trends in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire and connect the global tech community. Our mission is to promote technological innovation, foster collaboration, and provide a platform for experts to share insights and advancements in the industry.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, the founder of SpaceX and Tesla, shared his vision for the future of transportation and space exploration during his keynote at TechCon 2017. His contributions to electric vehicles and renewable energy have transformed multiple industries.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook, spoke at TechCon 2018 about leadership and the importance of empowering women in tech. Her insights into business and technology have made her a prominent figure in Silicon Valley.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, delivered a speech at TechCon 2019 on the future of AI and cloud computing. Under his leadership, Microsoft has become a leader in cloud services and artificial intelligence.
